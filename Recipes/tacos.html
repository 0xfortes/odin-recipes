Crispy Tacos

Description

These Crispy tacos with kiwi salsa are a unique, refreshing, and tasty meat-free snack

If you’re looking for delicious vegan snacks, then these crispy tacos with kiwi salsa are for you.

Ingredients:

  • 6 Taco Shells

    • Bean Filling:

  • 1x Onion
  • 2x Tomatoes
  • 1x Can Red Beans
  • ½ tsp Salt
  • ½ tsp Italian herbs
  • ¼ tsp Chili flakes
  • 1 tsp Olive oil

    • Kiwi Salsa

  • 1x Onion
  • 1x Handful of Parsley
  • 3-4x Kiwis
  • 1x Lemon Juice
  • ½ tsp Salt

Steps:

    Bean Filling

  1. In a pan heat up olive oil and saute finally chopped onion for about 3-4 minutes until slightly golden brown
  2. Add salt and chopped tomatoes and saute for another 5 minutes
  3. Add drained and rinsed kidney beans and start mashing them wit spatula while it's sauteing

    4. Kiwi salsa

  4. In a bowl add chopped onion, parsley and kiwi and mix with salt and lemon juice
  5. Leave it to marinate for about 5 minutes
  6. Now build your tacos by placing bean mixture first and then kiwi salsa
